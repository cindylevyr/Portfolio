About Me

Born and raised in Caracas, Venezuela. Cindy Levy R. has been in a love affair with the arts and leisure since childhood. While studying TV and Radio Production in NY she found a passion for music and playwriting and more important Advertising.

When she moved to Miami Cindy dove into The Miami International University of Arts and Design to study her new passion. Today she is known for being a very creative and avant-garde person who is willing to go to the extreme for an idea.
